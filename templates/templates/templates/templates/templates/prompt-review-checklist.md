# Prompt Review Checklist

A checklist for improving AI prompts before publishing or reusing them.

## Purpose

This checklist helps creators make prompts clearer, safer, and more repeatable.

## Checklist

### 1. Goal

- Is the goal of the prompt clear?
- Does the prompt explain what the AI should create?
- Is the expected output format included?

### 2. Context

- Does the prompt give enough background?
- Does it explain the audience or use case?
- Does it include platform-specific needs?

### 3. Style

- Is the visual, musical, or writing style described clearly?
- Are mood, tone, and pacing included?
- Are unnecessary references removed?

### 4. Constraints

- Does the prompt say what to avoid?
- Are there limits for length, format, or structure?
- Are risky or confusing instructions removed?

### 5. Reusability

- Can someone else reuse this prompt?
- Can the prompt be adapted for another project?
- Is the template easy to copy and modify?

## Final Review

A strong prompt should be clear, specific, reusable, and easy to adapt.
