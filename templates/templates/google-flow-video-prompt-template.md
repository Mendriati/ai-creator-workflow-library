# Google Flow Video Prompt Template

A reusable template for creating structured AI video prompts.

## Template

**Format:**  
16:9 cinematic video

**Scene:**  
Describe the main scene clearly.

**Subject:**  
Describe the main character, object, or focus.

**Mood:**  
Describe the emotional tone.

**Visual style:**  
Describe lighting, camera, color palette, and texture.

**Camera movement:**  
Describe the movement, such as slow push-in, tracking shot, or static frame.

**Avoid:**  
List things the model should not include.

## Example

A cinematic 16:9 video scene of a lone creator working late at night, surrounded by notebooks, music equipment, and a glowing laptop screen. Moody blue shadows, warm desk light, subtle film grain, realistic camera movement, calm and focused atmosphere.

Avoid clutter, fake UI, unreadable text, extra hands, horror, or overly futuristic neon style.
